## The Portfolio That Demonstrates Everything

At avivashishta.com, Avi built what is widely considered the most technically ambitious developer portfolio in the React/Next.js ecosystem: a site navigable entirely through hand gestures and head movement.

The tech stack: MediaPipe (real-time hand and head tracking via WebAssembly), Three.js + React Three Fiber (3D animations), React 19, TypeScript, GSAP, and Vite.

The result: move your hand in front of your webcam, and the cursor follows. Pinch to click. Tilt your head to scroll. The entire portfolio is accessible without touching mouse or keyboard.

"I wanted a portfolio that showed what I could build, not just listed it," Avi said. "If someone wants to know what kind of developer I am, I'd rather they experience it."
