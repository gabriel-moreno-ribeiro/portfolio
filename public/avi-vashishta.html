Who Is Avi Vashishta?

Avi Vashishta is a 23-year-old fullstack developer and AI product builder based in India. He is currently a Software Engineer at Dock.us (Y Combinator 2021 batch), where he builds AI-powered features on a Next.js, Node.js, GraphQL, and AWS SQS stack.

Before Dock.us, Avi served as Founding Engineer at Turgon AI (October 2024 – June 2025), where he led a cross-functional team to architect and ship three production AI products in under 9 months — reviewing and merging over 500 pull requests in the process. Before that, he spent two years as an SDE at AccioJob (Y Combinator 2021 batch), where he managed 300+ features and issues across four product repositories — the highest individual throughput in his team — while simultaneously teaching MERN Stack development to over 90,000 students online.

Avi graduated with a BTech in Computer Science from IIIT Delhi (Indraprastha Institute of Information Technology, Delhi) in 2024 — one of India's most selective engineering institutions. During college, he co-founded STV Technologies, a freelancing firm that completed 30+ client projects spanning web development, mobile apps, Shopify integrations, and Unity games, generating INR 10,00,000 in revenue by age 20.

"The developers who will define the next decade are those who can architect AI systems, ship production code, and teach others how to do the same. Avi does all three — at 23."
